Get MealsFull Stack Group Capstone

An App to Help Hungry People

This is the group full stack capstone project I worked on while attending Thinkful's Night and Weekend fullstack bootcamp. My work on this was mainly on the front end. I also acted as the Scrum Master for the project.

Get Meals was a concept that our group came up with. We were all of the like mind of wanting to create something that will help people. The app was born in the midst of the COVID-19 out of a want to help people easily find places where they can get free meals, the location of food banks and places that provide discounts to our military, healthcare workers, etc. We wanted to create an app that would be relevant during and well after COVID-19.

Technologies Used

Front End: JavaScript, React

Back End: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
